# MVP Goal

A desktop app for visualizing and exploring the conversations exported from an LLM chat app like Claude.

<https://support.claude.com/en/articles/9450526-how-can-i-export-my-claude-data>

> Follow these steps to export your data:
>   Click on your initials in the lower left corner of your account.
>   Select "Settings" from the menu.
>   Navigate to the "Privacy" section.
>   Click the "Export data" button.

## MVP

have a parser that can open an already uncompressed files: conversations.json

have a UI that can scroll up and down to display the "speaker" and "message"
